Dr. Mistriotis presented his work on Jan. 5 at the annual Biomedical Engineering Society-Cellular and Molecular Bioengineering (BMES-CMBE) conference in Carlsbad, California, where he was recognized as a 2025 BMES-CMBE Rising Star Junior Faculty Award winner.

The award recognizes researchers in their early independent career stages who have made an outstanding impact on the field of cellular and molecular bioengineering.
